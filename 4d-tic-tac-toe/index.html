4D Tic-Tac-Toe!
Use the LMB to take a turn in any cell, or tap if you're on a phone. You can collapse a cube to get a better perspective by using the checkbox next to its label. To win, you must get a win on all four dimensions (a 1D win would be a regular line, a 2D one a diagonal, 3D across boards and 4D across cubes).
